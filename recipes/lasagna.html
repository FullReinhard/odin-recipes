Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella sauce

Steps:

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  3. Repeat the same layers two more times.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese. Cover and bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at end if desired. Let rest for 15 minutes prior to serving with garlic bread.
