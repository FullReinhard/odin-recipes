Spring Greens and Bacon Sandwich Recipe

Description:

Appearing in Today's Menu for the Emiya Family's fourth episode, these sandwiches are the hallmark of simplicity, but do not lack for flavor or aroma. They can be enjoyed by just about anyone, whether they be an aloof highschool student or a vigilant Servant.

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 pcs. white bread
  • 1-1.5 bunches field mustard/greens
  • 8 eggs
  • 12 pcs. long bacon (16-18 if shorter than bread slices
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • [A] Spicy Mustard Butter
    • ~2-4g (0.07-0.14 oz) Japanese spicy mustard
    • 50g butter/margarine (1.75 oz)
  • [B] Sandwich Sauce
    • 4 tbsp. mayonnaise
    • 2 tbsp. coarse mustard
    • 2/3 tsp. honey
    • coarse pepper

Steps:

  1. Bring ingredients in [A] to room temp, then mix to create a spicy mustard spread for the bread. Mix the ingredients in [B] to create sauce for sandwiches.
  2. Boil field mustard for 1-2 minutes in salted water, then transfer to ice water. Dry off with paper towels once cooled. (! Don't keep greens in cold water too long, or they lose their flavor)
  3. Add salt to eggs, then scramble in frying pan. (Don't let eggs separate or clump too much)
  4. Cook bacon in large amount of oil over low-medium heat until brown and crispy. Transfer to paper towels once finished. Wipe off excess oil (! Have care, excess oil splatters easily)
  5. Spread spicy mustard butter over one side of a loaf of white bread, then add ingredients in following order from the bottom up: bacon, field mustard, [B] sauce, eggs, second loaf. Things to Note:
    1. Arrange bacon tighly so vegetables do not touch bread
    2. Placing crispier ingredients on the bottom to make sandwich easier to cut.
  6. Wrap sandwiches in plastic wrap. Lightly place flat object on top to hold sandwich in place for 5-10 minutes, allowing ingredients to mix. Slice and serve.
